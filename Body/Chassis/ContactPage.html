Meet the Maker

I'm /u/Eliminioa, Governor of the Midnight Marsh and 5th PAF Wing Commander. The original concept for this was inspired by Sahdee's strategy of messaging each Periwinkle she could a while back. After a few instances, I figured I'd whip up a bot to do the tedious work of sending out PMs. Originally a small but messy python script, it soon became a sprawling mess, tied to Periwinkle_Prime. Three incarnations later, Periwinkle_Prime_3 is sleeker, faster, and more modern. Still, under the purview of the Periwinkle Advanced Research Defense Agency, few were allowed to even access the bot. Recently, in an unprecedented leak, classified PADRA information was accessed by third-party hackers, rendering much of the secrecy obsolete. Left with bare shreds of dignity, PADRA officials decided to release the full code to  the public, and within a week the Orangered Nation had adopted its use. Despite the veil of secrecy being lifted, I've continued to work to make it more adaptable and powerful, and this web app is the culmination of that effort.
