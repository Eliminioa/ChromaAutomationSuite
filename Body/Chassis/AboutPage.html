About the CAS

As mentioned on the front page, the Chroma Automation Suite is designer to make managing Chroma easier and faster. It aims to  offer a suite of tools for the mods of both Periwinkle and Orangered, including its flagship feature, the alert messaging system.

Alert Messaging System

The CAS alert messaging system is the original, and arguably the primary feature of the CAS. It allows authorized mods, namely the generals of each side, to send messages to some or all of their commanders indicating the time and place of the next battle, as well as any other information. This is done through the web interface provided here.

CAS Web Interface

Lore Aggregation

The history of Chroma is extensive, comprising more that 400 posts in /r/Chromalore alone. This service works to collect all of that valuable history in one place. It sorts it into a living anthology, separated by author and by series. 

This feature is under construction.
